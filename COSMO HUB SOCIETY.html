Introduction

Humanity is witnessing significant advancememnts in space exploration,highlighting the necessity to establish independent food system that operate in unconventional environments, as sustainable food transport from Earth becomes challenging and impractical. Vertical farming emerges as one of possible solutions, allowing for crop cultivation in fully integrated series for stacked layers, using minimal spatial footprints.

Vision & Goals

Vision and Goals in this project

Vision

Vision: This initiative aims to develop a sustainable agricultural system in space by leveraging vertical farming techniques using soil-less agricultural systems. The vision is to achieve self-sufficiency in food supply for long-duration space missions, contributing to the sustainability of human life beyond Earth. By continuously improving farming technology, we aspire to overcome the challenges associated with harsh space environments and provide reliable and diverse food sources for

Goal 1

Develop Vertical Farming Technologies: Design innovative agricultural systems that enable the cultivation of a variety of crops in vertical formats, thereby optimizing the utilization of available space.

Goal 2

Enhance Resource Efficiency: Develop agricultural models that ensure optimal use of water and energy resources, while minimizing nutrient waste and increasing sustainability.
